# Student Earns $850/Month with ChatGPT Writing

## Introduction

Meet Zhang, a college student who discovered a profitable side hustle using ChatGPT. In just two months, he went from zero to earning $850 per month consistently.

## The Opportunity

Zhang noticed many people struggling with:
- Writing professional resumes
- Creating engaging social media content
- Drafting business emails and proposals

## The Method

### Step 1: Finding Clients
- Posted services on freelance platforms
- Offered competitive pricing ($10-30 per task)
- Focused on quick turnaround times

### Step 2: Using ChatGPT Effectively
- Learned to write detailed prompts
- Developed templates for common requests
- Always added personal touch and editing

### Step 3: Scaling Up
- Built a portfolio of satisfied clients
- Received referrals and repeat business
- Increased prices as reputation grew

## Income Breakdown

| Service | Price | Monthly Orders | Revenue |
|---------|-------|----------------|---------|
| Resume Writing | $25 | 15 | $375 |
| Social Media Copy | $15 | 20 | $300 |
| Business Emails | $10 | 18 | $180 |
| **Total** | | | **$855** |

## Key Tips

1. **Quality over speed** - Always review AI output
2. **Build relationships** - Happy clients refer others
3. **Specialize** - Focus on 2-3 service types
4. **Communicate clearly** - Set expectations upfront

## Conclusion

With minimal startup costs and flexible hours, ChatGPT writing services offer an excellent opportunity for students and beginners to start earning online.
